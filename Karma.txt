**Thought 1**
If one gives up all selfish desires and works merely for the sake of duty towards Supreme, 
then such work doesn't create any karmic reactions.

**Thought 2**
Do your duty but don't concern yourself with the fruits of your results.

**Thought 3**
Give up the pride of doership even while working.

**Thought 4**
The fruits of your actions are not for your enjoyment.

**Thought 5**
Do not be attached to inaction.
